Third, incorporate plenty of fresh vegetables and fruits into your diet. Fresh fruits and vegetables are packed with vitamins, minerals, antioxidants, and anti-inflammatory properties that can help improve psoriasis symptoms. Aim to get five to eight servings of fresh fruits and vegetables every day.

Omega 3 fatty acids are found in fish such as salmon, mackerel, and sardines, flaxseeds and chia seeds, and small amounts are found in walnuts.

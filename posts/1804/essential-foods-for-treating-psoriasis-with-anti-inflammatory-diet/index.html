Additionally, this diet may be deficient in certain nutrients. Moreover, there are also some nutrients that are found in foods that are excluded from the anti-inflammatory diet. Finally, in some cases, following an anti-inflammatory diet may require additional medications or supplements for balance and to prevent deficiencies. Therefore, psoriasis patients should consult with their physician or nutritionist to determine if this diet is appropriate for them, and to ensure they are getting all the necessary nutrients.

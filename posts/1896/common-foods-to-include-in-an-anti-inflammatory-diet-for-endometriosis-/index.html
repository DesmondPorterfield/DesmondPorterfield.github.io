1. Explanation of Endometriosis and its Symptoms#

Endometriosis is a condition in which the endometrial tissue which lines the uterus grows outside the uterus. Endometriosis can lead to severe pelvic pain, heavy or irregular menstrual cycles, and it can have a profound impact on a person’s quality of life. Common symptoms include pelvic pain, especially during menstruation, pain during intercourse, infertility, and fatigue. An anti-inflammatory diet can be beneficial for people with endometriosis, as it can reduce inflammation and related symptoms.

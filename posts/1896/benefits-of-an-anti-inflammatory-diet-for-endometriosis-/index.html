Endometriosis is a chronic health condition where endometrial tissue, which is the tissue that lines the inside of the uterus, grows outside of the uterus. An anti-inflammatory diet can help minimize the symptoms of endometriosis by removing potential triggers and nutrients that can cause the body to produce an inflammatory response.

Eliminating inflammatory foods, such as processed and refined sugars, trans-fats, and processed meats, can reduce inflammation and help minimize symptoms.

Q3. What kind of foods should be avoided on the anti-inflammatory diet? Foods that should be avoided on the anti-inflammatory diet include processed and refined foods, refined sugar, refined grains, fried foods, and foods high in saturated fat. Additionally, dairy products, red meat, and foods high in trans fats should be avoided.

Q4. What are some tips for starting an anti-inflammatory diet? Some tips for starting an anti-inflammatory diet include focusing on a variety of whole, unprocessed, nutrient-dense foods, such as fruits, vegetables, whole grains, legumes, nuts, and seeds. Additionally, it’s important to include plenty of healthy fats, such as olive oil and avocados, as well as lean proteins, like fish, poultry, and beans.

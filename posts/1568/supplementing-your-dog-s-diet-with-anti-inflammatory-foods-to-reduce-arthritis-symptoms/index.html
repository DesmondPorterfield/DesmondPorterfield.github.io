1. Overview of Arthritis Symptoms for Dogs#

Arthritis is a common health issue among older dogs, causing them discomfort, pain and in some cases, disability. Symptoms include limping, stiffness, difficulty going up and down stairs, lameness, reluctance to move, and general changes in attitude or behavior. A healthy diet and supplements that target inflammation can help reduce your dog’s arthritis pain.

One such supplement that may help is Omega-3 fatty acids. Found in fish oil and other sources, these oils are known to reduce inflammation and help with other joint-related issues. Make sure to look for products specifically for dogs to ensure your pup gets the right dosage.

Glucosamine and chondroitin are also both great options for aiding arthritis in dogs. Glucosamine helps stimulate the production of cartilage in your pup’s joints, while chondroitin works to reduce inflammation and cushion some of the pain.

If you suspect your pup may have arthritis, talk to your vet. He or she can help you develop a diet that works for your pet’s specific needs. You can also talk to them about supplements that may help reduce your pup’s pain and improve their quality of life.

Recent research suggests that following an anti-inflammatory diet may also help reduce the symptoms of certain autoimmune diseases. By limiting foods that trigger inflammation and eating more plant-based foods, people can help reduce their likelihood of experiencing autoimmune flares. Studies have shown that there is a link between the food we eat and the inflammation caused by our immune system.

Incorporating a variety of spices into your meals can not only provide a flavor boost but can also reduce chronic inflammation. Turmeric, ginger, cayenne pepper, and green tea are just a few of the anti-inflammatory ingredients that can help improve overall health.

Q1: What is an anti-inflammatory diet?

A1: An anti-inflammatory diet is a way of eating that is focused on eliminating pro-inflammatory foods and replacing them with anti-inflammatory foods. The goal of an anti-inflammatory diet is to reduce inflammation in the body which has been linked to several chronic diseases like heart disease, cancer, and diabetes.

Q2: What are some benefits of following an anti-inflammatory diet?

A2: Following an anti-inflammatory diet can help to reduce inflammation in the body. Reducing inflammation can lead to improved overall health, increased energy levels, improved digestive health, better immunity, a reduction in the risk of chronic diseases, and improved overall wellbeing.

Q3: What types of food are considered to be anti-inflammatory?

A3: Foods that are considered to be anti-inflammatory include fruits and vegetables (especially those high in antioxidants and polyphenols), fatty fish, nuts and seeds, herbs and spices, and teas.
